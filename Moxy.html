Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Moxy and how it works.

Due to Apple's iOS restrictions, third-party apps cannot send messages on your behalf automatically. Instead, Moxy sends you a notification at your scheduled time with your message ready to go — you just tap to send. This keeps your messages private and originating from your own number, with no third-party services involved.
Moxy supports SMS, iMessage, Apple Mail, Gmail, WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, Slack, Telegram, GroupMe, and more. Free plan users get Text and Email. Moxy Plus unlocks all platforms including WhatsApp and social apps.
Yes! Moxy Plus users can attach images, videos, PDFs, audio files, spreadsheets, and more to their scheduled messages. It's one of our most loved features — great for sending documents, photos, or important files at the right moment.
You'll get instant access to all Plus features the moment you upgrade. Your billing cycle starts from your upgrade date and you'll have full access to unlimited messages, all platforms, and file attachments right away.
Yes. We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied for any reason within 7 days of your purchase, contact us at moxymessenger@gmail.com and we'll make it right.
Absolutely. If you switch devices or reinstall the app, just reach out to our team and we'll restore your lifetime access — typically within minutes. We've done this many times and our users love how easy it is.
Moxy Plus includes a Birthday Reminders feature where you can link to your contacts' birthdays. Moxy will alert you in advance and can pre-populate a birthday message suggestion so you never miss wishing someone a happy birthday again.
Yes. Moxy processes everything on your device. Your messages are sent from your own phone number or email address — never through a third-party server. We don't read, store, or sell your messages. Your privacy is fundamental to how we built Moxy.
