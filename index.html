The Journey from Bully Walk

What started as Bully Walk at Mississippi State University—providing over 1.3 million navigation routes—has evolved into Gradway. Gradway continues to improve campus life by integrating maps, events, transit, and resources into a single seamless experience for students, staff, and visitors.

Why Campus Navigation Matters

Studies show that interactive campus maps reduce stress and help freshmen navigate large campuses efficiently, improving their overall college experience. Gradway empowers students to explore confidently, find study spots, and engage in campus life.
